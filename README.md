Social Media Campaign Performance Tracker (Task 2)

* Objective
Analyze Facebook and Instagram ad campaign data to measure performance using CTR, ROI, and engagement metrics.

* Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel

* Metrics Calculated
- Click Through Rate (CTR)
- Return on Investment (ROI)
- Engagement Rate

* Visualizations
- Clicks by Campaign
- ROI by Campaign
- CTR by Campaign

* Key Insights
- Instagram campaigns showed higher engagement and CTR.
- The New Product and Summer Sale campaigns delivered better ROI.
- Higher ad spend did not always lead to higher returns.

* Conclusion
This analysis helps identify effective campaigns and optimize future marketing strategies.
